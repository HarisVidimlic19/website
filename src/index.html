Real-Time 2D Solar System

This is a real-time snapshot of the Solar System showcasing the major celestial bodies. Their position from the Sun depend on the day of viewing. The data is taken from the NASA Horizons System and updated daily. The sizes of the planets (excluding the Sun) and distances from the Sun are accurate but scaled down by a factor of 108 for visualization purposes. The orbits of the planets are also accurate given their orbital elements and scaled down by the same distance factor.

Unique colours represent separate planets. Due to the visualization limitations and scale of certain page sizes, some bodies and orbits are hidden from the viewer. Outward from the center, the representations are as follows:

  • The Sun
  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune
